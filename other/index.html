Hobbies

I enjoy jogging and leetcoding in my free time. But most of all, I spent a lot of time getting lost in the world of video games.

My all time favorite game is Rainbow Six: Siege due to the amount of challenges it presents. Honing mechanical skills, strategic planning and execution, as well as maintaining a calm and collected mindset in the midst of stress and frustration, have all been crucial lessons that I never expected to learn in the context of video games.

The past few months I have spent almost exclusively on Baldur's Gate 3. Learning about the rules and settings of DnD was quite the fascinating experience. Currently my friends and I are working on an RNG playthrough, where we use a random number generator to select dialogue choices. Since it is quite tedious to switch to a different tab every time a choice is pending, I decided to write a randomizer program that can provide in-game overlay.
